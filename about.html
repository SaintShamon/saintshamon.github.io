About Michigan Nose & Sinus Health

Michigan Nose & Sinus Health is dedicated to improving the health and well-being of patients struggling with breathing, sinus and airway related issues. We are strongly committed to providing the very best one-on one care for our patients.

The practice is led by Dr. J. Martin Ulrich, D.O., F.O.C.O.O., a Board-Certified Otolaryngologist. With decades of experience, Dr. Ulrich is highly trained in medicine and surgery of the ear, nose, throat, head and neck as well as facial plastic and reconstructive surgery. Dr. Ulrich focuses on minimizing patient discomfort and accelerating recovery time for all aspects of ENT care. Our mission is to provide the highest quality medical care in a courteous, patient-friendly environment.

Meet Dr. Ulrich

What's in your best interest comes first with Dr. Ulrich. Patients describe him as easy to talk with, knowledgeable, and thorough.

He prefers a conservative course of care. However, when the situation calls for it, he is skilled in the very latest innovations and treatments, including surgery.

Dr. Ulrich possesses top credentials in his specialty. This means you get the very best of care.

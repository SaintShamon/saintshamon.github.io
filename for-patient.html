For Patients

Michigan Nose & Sinus Health strives to provide the highest level of service to our patients. To help make your first visit to our practice as smooth as possible, please read the information for Our Patients . You’ll find all the practical information you need, such as a map and directions to our offices, practice hours, payment policies and more. We also describe our first visit procedures.

Please bring the following to your appointment:

  • Insurance card (s) for patient
  • Photo ID
  • A list of your patients current medications
  • Referral, if required by insurance

Billing and Insurance

Some healthcare insurance plans require you to have a referral from your primary care physician prior to seeing a specialist. Also, many plans require preauthorization prior to surgeries or any surgical procedures. Our office will assist in initiating these referrals and pre authorizations, however it is the patient’s responsibility for any insurance denying payments for our services. We participate with most health insurance companies. For a comprehensive list of insurances or to see whether or not we participate with your insurance company, please contact our office at (810) 695-3766 .

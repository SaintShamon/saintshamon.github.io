Billing and Insurance

Some healthcare insurance plans require you to have a referral from your primary care physician prior to seeing a specialist. Also, many plans require preauthorization prior to surgeries or any surgical procedures. Our office will assist in initiating these referrals and pre authorizations, however it is the patient’s responsibility for any insurance denying payments for our services.

We participate with most health insurance companies. For a comprehensive list of insurances or to see whether or not we participate with your insurance company, please contact our office at (810) 695-3766.
